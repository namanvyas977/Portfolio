A Brief Summary

During my third year of undergrad, I conrinued my role of leading the robot design and calculations for documentation in the DD Robocon 2022 national competition. As the leader, I guided a sub-team in the designing process. The challenge in this competition was to build one or two robots according to the game rulebook, and the task was to play the traditional Indian street game called Lagori.

Our team's robot stood out and earned the Visvesvarya Best Design Award due to its unique design. What set our robot apart was its ability to carry five Lagori discs simultaneously, a feature that no other team had incorporated. This achievement showcased the effectiveness of our design approach and the innovative solutions we implemented.
